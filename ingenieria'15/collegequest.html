    Tech Quest
    Rules & Regulations
    (Engineering Students)

    LINE FOLLOWING ROBOT COMPETITION RULES strictly to be followed by each and every participating members(s) :-

    Objective: The science ideas should be innovative and unique.There should be a clear explanation of the project.
    ID Proof: The participants should bring their own institute ID card.
    Judges: The BIEMS will provide the judges for the competition. The decision of all officials regarding these rules and conduct of the event shall be final.
    Power: Each team will be provided with a 3 pin socket.
    Equipment: Bring your own electrical equipments and accessories (if any). There should be a chart paper display on the topic.
    Liability: BIEMS will have no liability for any damages done to the projects.
    Event Specification: The event will be held in BIEMS campus.Participants will be guided to the location accordingly.
    Team: The team must comprise of maximum 3 members.
    Winning Prize:
    1st Prize- Inkjet Printer
    2nd Prize-32 GB Pen drive
    3rd Prize-16 GB Pen drive
