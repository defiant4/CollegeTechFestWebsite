    Counter Strike 1.6
    Rules & Regulations

    Rules strictly to be followed by each and every participating player(s) :-

    1. TEAM MUST CONTAIN FIVE MEMBERS ONLY.Maximum of TWO specs/subs will be allowed.
    2. Map Selection A knife round in the Map de_dust2 will be the decider of the match.The winning team will have a choice of the following maps:- 1. de_dust2 2. de_inferno 3. de_nuke 4. de_train
    3. Map rounds Like every normal clan match , there will be 15 rounds per half , 2 halfs in all. As soon as a team wins 16 rounds in total , the match will end. All matches will be of best of 1 map.Final will be of best of 3 maps.
    4. In case of a tie:-For Knockout matches:- best of 3 rounds will happen (in continuation).
    5. Time Limit:- If the teams fail to reach on time, they will lose that particular match and a 16-0 win will be awarded to the opposite team .Maximum waiting time for the teams will be 10 minutes. At least 3 players should report within 10 minutes of the match.
    6. Prohibitions:-USE OF ANY CFG FILE IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED.SCROLL DUCK IS NOT ALLOWED. MAP BUGS ARE NOT ALLOWED. BOOSTING IN KNIFE ROUND IS RESTRICTED. 16-BIT COLOR QUALITY WOULD NOT BE ALLOWED. Only team chat is allowed.
    7.ALSO FOLLOWING WEAPONS ARE STRICTLY NOT ALLOWED:-
    I.BULLPUP(aug) II. KREIG 552(sg552) III. KREIG 550(sg550) IV. D3/AU-1(g3sg1)
    8. Communication :-Abusing will be tarted badly.The abuser will be warned for that particular round. Abusing authorities/admins/opponents while match will result in being disqualified.
    9.Entry Fee: Rs.50(for each BIEMS Student),&,Rs.80(for each outside player). During Registration full team amount has to be paid.In case of spectators/substitutes,extra of Rs.30 has to be paid for each member.

    N.B:-IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTE THE CO-ORDINATORS/ADMINS/AUTHORITIES' DECISION WILL BE THE FINAL DECISION. Be present at the time is given for the matches
