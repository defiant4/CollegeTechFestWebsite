    Robotics Rules & Regulations

    LINE FOLLOWING ROBOT COMPETITION RULES strictly to be followed by each and every participating members(s) :-

    Robot Weight: As per your convenience.
    Robot Dimensions: Any, as long as participated can handle the robot without assistance.
    Robot Control Specifications: The robot has to be completely autonomous. Once a robot has crossed the starting line it must remain fully autonomous, or it will be disqualified.
    Objective: Complete the course as fast as possible. If theres a tie between the fastest, a run-off will determine the winner. If nobody finish the run, the farthest one run on tracks will wins.
    Time Limit: There are a maximum of 3 tries for each robots. However, all 3 tries must not exceed 15 minutes. The fastest run of the 3 will count.
    Stopping: The team can decide to stop the robot anytime and get credited with the distance traveled along the line up until the robot stop.
    Losing the line: A robot that goes off of the line for over 30 seconds will be disqualified. A robot shall be deemed to have left the arena when all wheels, legs, or tracks have moved completely off the arena surface.
    Judges: BIEMS will provide the judges for the competition. The decisions of all officials regarding these rules and the conduct of the event shall be final.
    Power: Battery operated robots will be allowed. A 3 pin socket will be provided to each team.
    Equipment: Bring your own electrical equipments and accessories(if any).
    Liability: BIEMS will have no liability for any damages done to the robots.
    Course Specifications: The course is BIEMS Campus ,2nd floor. Participants will be guided to the location.
    Followings are some important points you need to know: The line shall be black, electrical tape traversing the arena from end to end.The arena has white tiles and white paper which used to cover the tiles.
    Team: A maximum of four members per team will be allowed. On winning: There will be one winner team which shall get a cash prize of Rs 5000. Besides, certificates will be given for all participants.

    Entry price: Rs.500 per group
